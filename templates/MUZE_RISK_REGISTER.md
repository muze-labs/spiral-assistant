# Muze Risk Register

## Purpose

This register tracks known risks, newly discovered risks, mitigation options, and evidence needed.

A risk can be product, technical, architectural, process, or AI-related.

## Risk levels

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Low | Worth watching, unlikely to block progress. |
| Medium | Needs active mitigation or evidence soon. |
| High | Could invalidate the roadmap or maturity target. |
| Critical | Should pause the normal roadmap until assessed. |
